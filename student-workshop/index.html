Overview

About This Meeting

Part of the Annual Research Exchange Series, this meeting brings together students from CHAI partner institutions for a half-day of presentations, discussions, and collaborative research planning.

1Research Presentations

Students present their ongoing work in AI-enhanced education, learning analytics, and human-AI interaction.

2Peer Feedback

Structured peer review sessions to receive constructive feedback from fellow researchers across institutions.

3Idea Incubators

Round table discussions to brainstorm new research directions and identify potential collaboration opportunities.

4Networking

Build cross-institutional relationships with peers working at the intersection of education and technology.

Format

Meeting Structure

The meeting is designed to maximize interaction and collaborative learning among student researchers.

Thematic Keynote

Opening presentation on the year's spotlight theme at the intersection of pedagogy, technology, and cognitive science.

Paper & Poster Presentations

Students present their research through oral presentations and poster sessions with Q&A.

Round Table Discussions

Small-group discussions on emerging challenges and collaborative research planning across institutions.

Collaborative Planning

Sessions to identify shared research interests and plan cross-institutional student projects.
