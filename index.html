About CHAI

Our Research Vision

Building co-evolutionary ecosystems for human-AI collaborative learning, bridging theory, system design, and real-world implementation.

Interdisciplinary Research

Crossing AI, education, cognitive science, psychology, and design to understand co-creative learning between humans and AI.

International Collaboration

A global network connecting researchers, engineers, and practitioners through visiting units, joint labs, and exchange programs.

Social Implementation

Partnering with schools, businesses, and local governments to translate research into real-world educational impact.
